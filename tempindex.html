On Jupiter's moon Io, the ANDOR Corporation has been manufacturing heavy machinery and robot parts for all of the major Houses in the solar system. But during the latest skirmish between House Calypso and the Ganymede Steadfast, ANDOR's corporate headquarters suddenly went dark. Comms in Epaphos City are dark and that entire section of the Jovian grid has gone offline.


You are an agent of the Helios Authority, the watchdog group that polices the separate but confederated Houses on all the worlds of the Sol planetary system. You have been sent on a mission to discover what is going on in Epaphos City, what has happened to the ruling council there, and what the disposition is of ANDOR's corporate warlords.


The job is not without danger. After all, you are the twelth agent to be dispatched to this moon. The others have never returned . . .
